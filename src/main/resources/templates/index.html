Our Story

The importance of software in Cyber security

Cyber security is a field where everyone have to work with software specially related to one direction. It can Penetration testing, Log analysis, SecOps, so on. Sometimes it is difficult to install or keep updated a piece of software. In some cases it is a laborious work, in other: people are not aware of better alternatives. Our solutions is to make Cyber security specialist happier and more productive with their job by automating the process of installation and configuration of their favorite program.
