Our Story

The importance of software in Cyber security

Cyber security is a field where everyone have to work with software specially related to one direction. It can Penetration testing, Log analysis, SecOps, so on. Sometimes it is difficult to install or keep updated a piece of software. In some cases it is a laborious work, in other: people are not aware of better alternatives. Our solutions is to make Cyber security specialist happier and more productive with their job by automating the process of installation and configuration of their favorite program. Use Breath OS and try out a suitcase - a package of software specially designed for you. Need more? Give suggestions and ideas to our forum.

Powerful packages

Suitcases - are packages that contain everything for a specific major or industry. Our suitcases are oriented to cyber security specialists of different categories. For each category there is a package that is designed to meet the needs of the specialist and you can find them in BStore.

All in one OS

Stop to download different variations of Linux distributions with different sets of programs. Just download Breath OS and you will have a chance to use the programs that you want. You only have to select suitable to you suitcase.

Community support

This is community driven project that is why we are always looking for new contributors. Are you a tool developer? May be you are working in a a company that creates own security software? Are you just a cyber security specialist? Just post in our forum, and we will help to you accelerate your projects and shape our suitcases to make them better.

The best for learners

This project can support not only professionals of cyber security careers, but also beginners. Now, the list of professional tools is clear! Start to learn how to use it right now, without any problems. No red eyes and black terminals.
